Trigger 1: change_availability_rooms

This trigger runs before a new row is inserted into the LocatedIn table and has two main purposes:

  • To check if the room has reached its maximum capacity.
  • To update the room's availability status if it becomes full.

Case 1: Room has available space

The room is not yet full. Inserting a patient should succeed without changing the room's availability.

Please enter Patient ID.
Please enter Room ID.
Try: 572697154, 6

Case 2: Room becomes full after adding this patient

The room has one spot left. After insertion, the room will be full and its availability will be set to 0.

Please enter Patient ID.
Please enter Room ID.

Case 3: Room is already full

Trying to add another patient to a room that has already reached its maximum capacity.
